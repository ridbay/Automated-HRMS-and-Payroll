Based on an in-depth analysis of Pandar’s operational documents, standard operating procedures (SOPs), and governance frameworks, you have a phenomenal data model to build a highly robust, enterprise-grade HR Management System (HRMS).

To make your system uniquely valuable for Pandar and highly scalable for other companies, you should move beyond basic "employee profile" tracking and build modules that directly automate complex corporate workflows.

Here are the key modules and specific features you should implement:

---

## 1. Automated Onboarding & Offboarding Orchestration

Pandar has highly detailed, multi-step workflows for joining and leaving the company. Your system should act as a workflow engine that enforces these policies dynamically.

* 
**Pre-Employment Verification Pipeline:** A step-by-step gatekeeper that tracks mandatory background checks (Identity, Employment, Reference, Address) and flags conditional ones (Academic, Professional, Risk-Based Criminal Check). It should prevent the next stage if checks are "Unsatisfactory".


* 
**The "No NDA, No Access" Hard Gate:** Automate document issuance (Offer Letter, Contract, NDA, Biodata Form). A critical feature would be a backend blocker: **system access provisioning is strictly locked** until the digital signature on the NDA is verified by the system.


* 
**Cross-Departmental Offboarding Task Boards:** When a separation is initiated , the system should automatically generate a shared, dependency-mapped checklist across **IT** (Access Revocation), **Admin** (Asset Return), **Finance** (Financial Cleared), and **HR** (File Archived). Final clearance is only granted when all departmental tokens match "Complete".



---

## 2. Dynamic IT Access & Asset Management Matrix

Modern HR systems must bridge the gap between human resources and IT infrastructure (especially for ISO 27001 compliance, which Pandar adheres to).

* 
**Role-Based Access Control (RBAC) & Principle of Least Privilege:** A dashboard allowing Hiring Managers to request system access configurations based entirely on job roles.


* 
**Access Verification Reminders:** Build a compliance cron-job that forces line managers to verify that a new employee’s access matches their role within exactly **five (5) business days** of onboarding.


* 
**Asset Lifecycle Register:** Track company property (Laptops, Mobile Devices, Access Cards) from issuance to return. Include a **Depreciation & Ownership Tracker**: trigger an alert when a laptop hits its **3-year usage milestone**, flagging the employee as eligible for an ownership transfer upon exit.


* **Shared Liability Cost Calculator:** In the asset module, create a portal to log damaged/lost items. Program the system to calculate deduction amounts automatically: **50/50 split** for accidental damage or **100% employee liability** for proven negligence.



---

## 3. Strict Compliance Time & Attendance Module

Pandar enforces strict parameters on working hours, lateness, and reporting protocols.

* **The "10:10 AM" Grace-Period Clock:** A hybrid check-in system (onsite/remote) that automatically logs status. Since resumption is 10:00 AM with a 10-minute grace period, anyone checking in at **10:11 AM or later is automatically tagged as "Late"** in the database.


* 
**Three-Day Job Abandonment Trigger:** If an employee logs zero attendance or communication for **three consecutive working days**, the system should automatically lock their profile and ping HR with a "Deemed Job Abandonment / Termination Review" alert.


* 
**Missing Report Penalty & Fine Ledger:** Pandar charges a **₦50,000 fine** for unsubmitted monthly reports. Your platform should feature a document upload portal for Monthly Reports. If the deadline passes without an upload, the system automatically appends a ₦50,000 deduction parameter to that month's payroll module and fires a warning. On the 3rd strike, it triggers a disciplinary query.



---

## 4. Complex Leave Engine with Embedded Business Rules

Standard HR systems fail at handling complex corporate leave rules. Building Pandar's specific constraints will make your software incredibly robust.

* 
**Proportional Annual Leave Roster:** Entitle confirmed users to 15 days (hardcoded as 10 rest days and 5 sick days). Force a rule where employees must pre-schedule **at least 70% of their leave** at the start of the leave year, leaving 30% floating. Add a hard reset on November 30th that automatically forfeits unused days (no rollovers, no cash-outs).


* **Maternity Pay Tiering Logic:** Create an automated payroll logic engine for maternity leave:
* 
*If Tenure $\ge$ 1 Year & Confirmed:* 100% Gross Pay for 16 weeks (minus feeding/transport allowances).


* 
*If Tenure $<$ 1 Year or Unconfirmed:* 45% Gross Pay.


* 
*Frequency Gate:* Block full-pay maternity or paternity applications if they occur within **24 months** of a previous claim (dropping pay to 45% if breached).


* 
*Resumption Block:* Freeze the "Annual Leave" button for precisely **6 months** post-maternity resumption.




* 
**Conflict Prevention Scheduling:** Prevent employees from the same organizational unit/team from taking annual leave simultaneously by cross-checking the team schedule before approving a request.



---

## 5. Dual Framework Career Progression & Succession Planner

This will be your system's most premium feature. Pandar utilizes two separate metrics: *Performance* (what you achieve) and *Competency* (how ready you are for responsibility).

* 
**The 50/15/20/15 KPI Scorecard:** Build a performance appraiser based exactly on Pandar’s custom weighted structure:


1. 
*KPIs & Projects:* 50% 


2. 
*Learning & Development:* 20% 


3. 
*Timely Execution:* 15% 


4. 
*Behaviour & Communication:* 15% 




* **Matrix-Based Promotion Eligibility Engine:** Instead of manual tracking, create a "Promotion Readiness" dashboard. An employee only unlocks the "Eligible for Promotion" badge if the database confirms they meet all conditions simultaneously:


* Tenure $\ge$ 12 months at current level.


* Average KPI score $\ge$ 70% across two consecutive cycles.


* Competency Proficiency Scale average $\ge$ 3 out of 5.


* Active disciplinary queries = 0.




* 
**High-Potential (HiPo) & Succession Pipeline:** A calibration tool mapping performance against leadership readiness indicators. Allow HR to map potential internal "Successors" to critical roles to ensure business continuity.



---

## 6. Regulatory Audit Support & Security Vault

To attract other high-growth tech companies and finance startups, your HRMS must prioritize data integrity and security.

* **Three-Query Disciplinary Tracker:** A corrective action module that logs warnings. The system should track the progressive steps automatically: First Query $\rightarrow$ Second Query $\rightarrow$ Third Query (Final Warning) $\rightarrow$ Suspension/Termination Review. It should also feature a "Gross Misconduct Bypass" allowing admins to jump straight to termination for severe offenses.


* 
**ISO 27001 Log Generator:** Maintain immutable log records of onboarding checklists, asset assignments, NDA records, and signed policy acknowledgments (Handbook, Remote Work Policy, Acceptable Use). This provides instant exportable compliance evidence for external auditors.


* 
**Secure Record Disposal Engine:** A retention scheduler that flags archived employee data when its legal/corporate lifecycle ends, prompting HR to execute a recorded "Secure Deletion" protocol with an entry into the Record Disposal Log.
